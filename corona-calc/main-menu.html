Welcome to CoronaFiles.us

We have made these tools to help visualize and understand the progression of the COVID-19 pandemic in the United States. Tools are designed to report US Corona Virus data at a local level (state and county). To get started click a link for the tool you want to explore.

Alerts

Counties across the United States that are experiencing more than 40% new case growth in the last 7-days. Use this tool to spot early outbreaks or counties where the virus is spreading fastest.

Hotspots

US Counties with the largest new case volume per day (Counties with > 100 new cases per day)

Herd Immunity Calculator

What is the cost of herd immunity in time and lives? Use this calculator to estimate the impact of herd immunity on a state or county.

International Comparison

Wondering how Sweden compares with the United States, New York or your county? Use this tool to compare cases per million and deaths per million between any two countries in the world.

Forecast

When and how will corona virus end? Find out how your state or county is doing? This tool uses a linear projection to determine if the virus spread can be stopped or if herd immunity will set in.
